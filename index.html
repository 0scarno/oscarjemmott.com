// üìù about //

  • Hello, I'm Oscar, a Brisbane based audio designer who creates original music and sound for media, with a focus on interactivity. I enjoy working in creative and non-conventional ways, in order to represent the essential character of a work.

    Collaboration is an integral part of my practise. I always strive to work closely with others to understand, develop and enhance the core of each project I'm involved with.

    I hold a Bachelor of Music (With Distinction) from QUT, Majoring in Music and Sound Production with a mixed minor focussing on sound design and interaction. I've enjoyed working on animations, films, and games in all kinds of styles.
