  • Hello, I'm Oscar, a Brisbane based composer, sound-designer and musician who creates original music and sound for games,films, and theatre. I enjoy working in creative and non-conventional ways, in order to represent the essential character of a work.

    I'm always striving to bring life to media through detailed and thoughtful audio. I'm passionate about creating cohesive and rich sounds which enhance the listener's experience and their connection to the work.

    I hold a Bachelor of Music (With Distinction) from QUT, Majoring in Music and Sound Production with a mixed minor focussing on sound design and interaction. I've enjoyed working on animations, films, and games in all kinds of styles.
